Track down who followed and unfollowed your or someone else's Instagram account right on your WordPress dashboard.

== Description ==

Get constantly updated ZebraBoss service statistics of followed and unfollowed followers on Instagram directly from your WordPress dashboard.
<ul>
<li>Do you want to know people who followed and unfollowed from your or someone else's <strong>Instagram account</strong>? Just add this plugin to your WordPress and get the free notofications!</li>
<li>Quick access to the followed and unfollowed followers report;</li>
<li>Instant notifications.</li>
</ul>

== Installation ==

1. You can either use the automatic plugin installer or your FTP program to upload it to your wp-content/plugins directory;
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress;
3. Visit your Instagram Followers Tracker options (Settings --> IFT Settings);
4. Enter your email used to subscribe to Zebraboss reports or the Zebraboss report URL and press "Save settings" button;
5. Everything's ready! Go to the Dashboard of your Wordpress and see the detailed report about the followed and unfollowed Instagram followers!
